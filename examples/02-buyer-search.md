# Buyer search for a company

Find likely acquirers for a target, ranked by fit and deal history. Exercises
`creating-tables` with `entity_type: buyer`.

## Prompt

```text
Find likely acquirers for a UK-based industrial MRO distributor doing ~£40M
revenue. Prioritize strategics and PE platforms with relevant deal history in
the last 5 years.
```

## What it demonstrates

- Building a buyer table from a target-company profile.
- Buyer geography and deal/investment-history filters.
- Distinguishing strategic vs. financial buyers with supporting evidence.
